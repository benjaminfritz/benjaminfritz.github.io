About

An overview of the different stations that formed my experience.

  • 2006 — 2009

    Bachelor of Arts in
    Communication Design

    Over the course of 3.5 years I studied at the University of Applied Sciences Schwaebisch Gmuend and finished it with specializing in Interaction Design from the 3rd semester onwards with a B.A. degree.

  • 2009

    Ogilvy CommonHealth Spain

    During my Erasmus semester in Madrid, Spain I did a 3-month internship in the healthcare unit of Ogilvy where my tasks involved animation, motion design, banner advertising and editing promotional videos mostly for Cialis.

  • 2010 — 2013

    Jung von Matt/Neckar

    Starting as an intern in 2008 I worked my way up from Junior Art Director straight after graduating to Art Director Online at Jung von Matt in Stuttgart with clients like Nikon, Kabel Deutschland, Bitburger, Bosch, Mercedes-Benz and Payback.

  • 2013 — 2014

    Tweek

    At Tweek my main responsibility was the UX & UI of the showcase iOS app but after time I was also helping out in various frontend tasks like creating and maintaining the weekly newsletter, editing the main landing page and creating responsive microsites for sub-brands of the company.
