PENNY Grillaktion

A teaser page to get people excited about the upcoming BBQ season.

One of my first projects for Parasol Island involved creating a teaser website for the BBQ products and events of German supermarket chain PENNY.

The campaign features German BBQ champion and vice world champion Michael Hoffmann and offered competitions, events and products around one of the Germans most favorite hobbies.

  • Date: March 2015
  • Client: Parasol Island
  • Category: Campaign Website

alphaEOS Redesign

Responsive redesign of the smart home appliance company website.

Early 2015 I was tasked with the redesign of the company website for alphaEOS, a little company in Southern Germany who wants to have a share in the smart home hype with their own custom-built solutions.

After some initial layouts for the responsive homepage, some subpages and custom illustrations for teaser slots they decided to switch from their hired freelancer task force to a full-service agency so the project unfortunately was never completed.

  • Date: March 2015
  • Client: alphaEOS
  • Category: Responsive Website

Bosch Smart Home

Support for the product launch of smart home devices for a big German brand.

Earlier this year Scholz & Friends Berlin hired me to support their inhouse designers with the launch of a new product category for German rooted technology and services supplier Bosch.

Over the course of two weeks we created the Smart Home landing page, a device configurator and detail pages.

  • Date: January 2015
  • Client: Scholz & Friends Berlin
  • Category: Product Website
